Undergraduate Program

About the Undergraduate Program

This undergraduate program in Computer Science and Engineering provides a comprehensive education in computer science fundamentals, focusing on areas like algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and systems design. The curriculum is structured to give students a balanced knowledge of theory and practical applications.
