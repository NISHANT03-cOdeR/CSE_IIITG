Welcome to IIIT Guwahati

Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati (IIITG) is an institution of National Importance under an Act of Parliament (THE INDIAN INSTITUTES OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (PUBLIC-PRIVATE PARTNERSHIP) ACT, 2017). It offers B.Tech. courses in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) and Computer Science Engineering (CSE), M.Tech. courses in CSE and ECE and runs PhD programmes in ECE, CSE, Mathematics, and Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS). IIITG started operations in August 2013 with B.Tech programmes in CSE and ECE. The first batch of B.Tech. students completed their programme in May 2017. The first convocation of the Institute was held on May 15 2018. The institute has further received funding for infrastructural development and academic improvement under TEQIP III. The Institute has shifted to its permanent campus in July 2018. Students are staying in the hostels inside the campus. Most of the faculty members are staying in the faculty residence within the campus itself. The campus is located on a 67 acre plot, 5 km from Guwahati airport, on the National Highway.

Placements at IIIT Guwahati

IIIT Guwahati had a successful placement session in 2023-24 with an average package of 14.88 lakhs per annum (approximately). Some of the companies where our students got placed are Amazon, Atlassian, Blackbasil, Cisco, D. E. Shaw, Deloitte, DeltaX, Devtron, Exeevo, Flipkart, GE Digital, Hugosave, MAQ, Microsoft, Mindtickle, Morgan Stanley, Nvidia, Samsung, TA Digital, TCS, Unistring, Versa Network, ZS, ZScalar. The selection process for some companies is still going on.

Accommodation & Facilities

The Boys’ hostel of IIIT Guwahati can accommodate 849 students and Girls’ hostel can accommodate 179 students. Students will either be in a double room (parallel beds) without attached toilet facilities, or a double room (parallel beds) with attached toilet facilities or in a double room with a double-decker bed with attached toilet facilities.

Sports & Recreation

A healthy mind resides in a healthy body. Sports and games are a major aspect of complete development both physically and mentally for a person. In this competitive age, sports is equally essential to supplement and enhance the academic development. The students at IIITG indulge in a number of sporting activities like basketball, football, badminton, etc. which inculcate in them a spirit to participate, compete and win. IIIT Guwahati has its annual techno-cultural-sports fest named “Yuvaan” to give equal opportunities to boys and girls to portray their skills in various fields of recreation and sports
