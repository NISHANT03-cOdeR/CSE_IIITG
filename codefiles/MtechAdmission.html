Admissions

Mtech

IIIT Guwahati welcomes prospective students for admission into the M.Tech CSE programme. We offer two modes of programmes, i.e. On-Campus and Online, to be suited for candidates with BE/B.Tech degree in Engineering/ Technology/ equivalent program OR M.Sc. (Physics/Mathematics/ Electronics/ Computer Science/ Information Technology/ Statistics/Bio-Informatics or equivalent) OR MCA from a recognized Institution. In the On-Campus mode of instruction, the students have to attend the regular classes physically within the campus, whereas in the online mode, the student can attend the classes in "online mode" from anywhere. For more details and eligibility criteria, please visit On-Campus or Online.
